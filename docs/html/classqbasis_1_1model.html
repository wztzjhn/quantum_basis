calculate dynamical structure factors

\[ G_A(z) = \langle \phi | A_q^\dagger (z-H)^{-1} A_q | \phi \rangle, \]

where \( z = \omega + E_0 + i \eta \), and norm = \( \sqrt{\langle \phi | A_q^\dagger A_q | \phi \rangle} \).

\[ G_A(z) = \frac{\langle \phi | A_q^\dagger A_q | \phi \rangle}{z-a_0 - \frac{b_1^2}{z-a_1 - \frac{b_2^2}{z-a_2 - \cdots}}} \]

on exit: \( a_i \), \( b_i \) and norm are given.

\[ A_q | G(Q_0) \rangle = \sum_i \frac{p_i}{N} | \varphi_i (Q_0 + q) \rangle, \]

where \( | G(Q_0) \rangle \) is the variational ground state.

Note: in this input, will use

\[ B_q \equiv \sqrt{N} A_q = \sum_i A_{r_i} e^{i q \cdot r_i}, \]

since we cannot directly work with \( N \rightarrow \infty \). (summation only over the box)

Also, we output \( p_i \) instead of \( p_i/N \) for the same reason.

Build the matrix for calculating the observables in the Wannier state

\[ \mu_{k_1 k_2} = \langle \phi(k_1) | B_{k_1 - k_2} | \phi(k_2) \rangle , \]

where

\[ B_q = \sqrt{N} A_q = \sum_i e^{i q \cdot r_i} A_{r_i}. \]

Note: \( \phi(k_1) \) and \( \phi(k_2) \) should be in the same band (same quantum numbers and well-defined dispersion). Thus, this function ONLY works if you really have a well defined band.

Each pair denotes the position \( r_i \) (in cartesin coordinates) and the operator \( A_{r_i} \).
